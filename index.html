About Us

Plastic recycling is an essential piece of the puzzle for better waste management & a truly circular plastic economy. Plastic recycling saves up to 90% of CO2 emissions. Plastic recycling keeps resources in the loop.

We Buy and Sell Recycled Plastic
We deal with marketing all types of post-industrial and post-consumer plastic waste. Greccon INC deals with the purchase and sale of selected post-industrial and post-consumer plastic waste.

Through our consultancy, purchase and sale of recycled plastics, our medium and long-term goal is to help reduce the production of new plastics, favoring the reuse and recycling of plastic waste produced by industries.

We aim to achieve this by facilitating the match between supply and demand of companies. We contact the industries that must get rid of plastic waste and the industries that, instead, use them as raw material.

Materials

We handle various plastic materials such as PA (Polyamide), PC (Polycarbonate), PE (Polyethylene), PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), PS (Polystyrene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and PVO (Polyvinyl Alcohol).

Our goal is to efficiently recycle and repurpose these materials, contributing to a sustainable future and reducing environmental impact. By partnering with industries, we ensure that these plastics are properly managed and given a second life.
